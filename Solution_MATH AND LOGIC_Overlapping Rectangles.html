Second solution

We are adding 333 squares of area 161616 and 333 squares of area 444.

Hence the answer is 3×16+3×4=48+12=603\times 16+ 3\times 4 = 48+ 12 =\textcolor{#3D99F6}{\boxed{60}}3×16+3×4=48+12=60​.

Since the larger L-shape is congruent to the blue region, it has an area of 484848, and since the ratio of the sides of smaller L-shape to the larger L-shape is 12\frac{1}{2}21​, the ratio of their areas is 1222=14\frac{1^2}{2^2} = \frac{1}{4}2212​=41​, which means the smaller L-shape has an area of 14⋅48=12\frac{1}{4} \cdot 48 = 1241​⋅48=12.
